Meet Your Barber

Marcus "Marc" Rodriguez

With over 15 years of experience in the craft of barbering, I've dedicated my career to perfecting the art of men's grooming. What started as an apprenticeship in a traditional New York barbershop has evolved into a passion for blending classic techniques with contemporary styles.

At Iron & Blade, I believe every man deserves to look and feel his absolute best. Whether you're looking for a classic cut, a modern fade, or a meticulous beard sculpting, I bring precision, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence to every chair.

I've trained under master barbers across the country, earned certifications in advanced cutting techniques, and continuously stay updated with the latest trends while honoring timeless barbering traditions.
